About Us

Leading Supplier of [Plastic Machinery Solutions]

Our Story

Elolimy was established in 1994 under the name Hazem Hassan Al-Olimy Plastic Industries in the Industrial Zone (C1), 10th of Ramadan City, Egypt.

Today, Elolimy operates as a specialized import and export office headquartered at Doha Mall, 10th of Ramadan City, and acts as an agent for leading Chinese manufacturers of plastic production lines and machinery.

We supply a wide range of plastic machines including injection molding, extrusion, blow molding, film production, and recycling systems, in addition to plastic molds, spare parts, and full factory equipment.

Elolimy proudly exports complete production solutions to more than 10 countries, supporting factories with reliable, high-performance industrial solutions.

Our Journey

A Timeline of Growth
& Industrial Excellence

Foundation Year 1994

El Olimy was established in 1994 in the Industrial Zone C1, 10th of Ramadan City, as a specialized company in importing and supplying industrial machines and packaging solutions.

Starting with a vision to deliver reliable industrial machinery solutions in Egypt.

Expansion Phase 1998

The company expanded its operations by importing advanced plastic processing machines and strengthening partnerships with international manufacturers.

Building strong technical and commercial partnerships abroad.

Production Solutions 2005

Introduction of complete production lines for plastic and packaging industries, offering integrated solutions for factories.

From machines to complete industrial production systems.

Technical Support Growth 2010

Strengthening after-sales service, spare parts supply, and technical support to ensure maximum machine performance.

Reliable support became a key part of our identity.

Modern Solutions 2015

Introduction of modern technologies in extrusion, injection, and recycling machines to meet global manufacturing standards.

Adapting to modern industrial evolution and automation.

Regional Expansion 2020 - Present

Expanding services across the Middle East and Africa, providing integrated packaging and industrial machinery solutions for large-scale factories.

A trusted name in industrial machinery and packaging solutions.
